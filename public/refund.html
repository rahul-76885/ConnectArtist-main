Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear and fair policies to ensure trust and transparency.

1. General Policy

At ConnectArtist, we aim to provide a seamless experience. Since our platform connects users with artists, cancellations and refunds follow the rules below.

2. Cancellations

Users may request cancellations within the allowed time frame before the scheduled service. Any request made after this period may not qualify for a refund.

3. Refund Eligibility

  • If a service is not delivered as promised, a full refund will be provided.
  • If the artist cancels the engagement, users are entitled to a full refund.
  • User-initiated cancellations depend on timing and may include deductions for processing fees.

4. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days using the original payment method.

5. Exceptions & Dispute Resolution

Certain services, once delivered (such as completed performances or digital content), may not be eligible for refunds. In case of disputes between users and artists, ConnectArtist will act as a mediator, and the final decision will be made at the sole discretion of the platform.

6. Contact Us

If you have refund or cancellation queries, please contact us via our Contact Page or email us at shubhamag1412@gmail.com.
